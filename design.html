What is Hand-Lettering & Design calligraphy ?

Hand Lettering has a completely different form from the usual writing rules, which is to draw the letters and decorate them, very different from writing them.

Features :

When drawing Hand Lettering types, the letters you control are simpler, completely different from Calligraphy. That's what makes Hand Lettering the perfect alternative to projects that are oversized to calligraphy, such as blackboards or other types of signage.

Method :

Uses the principles of down-to-light for strokes, but instead of being recorded with a brush and ink, Hand Lettering is usually written only with a quill pen. I usually use Sharpies, or brush-tip pens or water-based pens to write all kinds of letters.
